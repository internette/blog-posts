<h2>The Goals</h2>
The first goal is the convert the CodePen into a web application. The purpose of converting the pen into a full web app is to create a shareable experience. The user could share any card that they create. Upon finishing, the user would choose if the card should be public or private The landing page would be a gallery of public cards. Eventually, the user would be able to upload their own images as a background image.
